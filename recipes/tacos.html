Tacos recipe

Description

Tacos are a beloved Mexican dish that has gained popularity worldwide for its delicious flavors and versatility. This iconic meal typically consists of a soft or hard tortilla filled with a flavorful mixture of seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, cheese, and various toppings. In this recipe, seasoned ground beef or turkey forms the savory base of the taco filling, cooked with aromatic taco seasoning until rich and flavorful. The warm tortillas provide a soft and comforting vessel for the vibrant toppings, including crisp shredded lettuce, juicy diced tomatoes, zesty onions, and creamy shredded cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or turkey
  • 8-10 small tortillas
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning mix
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks
  2. Drain excess fat from the skillet, then add the taco seasoning mix and water according to the package instructions (usually around 3/4 cup of water).
  3. Simmer the meat mixture for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is reduced and the flavors are well combined.
  4. While the meat is cooking, warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave according to package instructions.
  5. Serve the tacos with optional toppings such as sour cream, salsa, guacamole, and lime wedges on the side
