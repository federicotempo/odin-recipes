Fried chicken Recipe

Description

Fried chicken is a classic dish beloved for its crispy exterior and juicy, flavorful interior. To prepare fried chicken, start by marinating chicken pieces in buttermilk to tenderize and flavor them. Then, coat the chicken in a seasoned flour mixture, typically including salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and optionally cayenne pepper for heat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken
  2. Marinate the Chicken
  3. Prepare the Coating
  4. Coat the Chicken
  5. Heat the Oil
  6. Fry the Chicken
  7. Drain and Serve
